Covid -19 is a global emergency in most countries which has brought about a negative impact in most of the busines and education sectors.There has been closure of most businesses and education sectors which has led to idleness among the youth.

Practices The Youth Can Engage In During This Pandemic

Using social media and apps to spread accurate information on how the pandemic is spread or else contacted and how to curb it,innovating to improve access to live saving testing,championing mental wellness(safeguarding peoples mental health), conducting research(this applies to the youths who are well equitted with Information Technology knowledge and skillls,engaging in delivery of foods and sanitization,ensuring the most vulnerable are not left behind through creating awareness in the form of groups so as to reach out for those who are not able to access social media platforms.)
